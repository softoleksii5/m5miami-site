Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

This website, m5miami.com (“the Site”), is operated by M5 Interior Design & Build (“M5”, “we”, “us”), an interior design, renovation and construction studio working in Miami, Florida. This policy explains what information we collect through the Site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information you give us

When you fill out a form on the Site — a contact form, the instant estimate, a project inquiry, or the request for our cost guide — you may provide your name, email address, phone number, neighborhood, budget range and details about your project. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry, prepare estimates, send you the materials you requested, and communicate about your project. Form submissions are delivered to our email via FormSubmit, a third-party form processing service.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we use analytics tools that collect usage data via cookies and similar technologies — pages visited, approximate location, device and browser type, and how you interact with the Site:

  • Google Analytics 4 — traffic and usage statistics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Microsoft Clarity — anonymized session replays and heatmaps that help us improve the Site (Microsoft Privacy Statement)

If you book a call through the Site, scheduling is handled by Google Calendar appointment booking; if you contact us via the WhatsApp buttons, your communication is governed by WhatsApp's own terms and privacy policy.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide estimates and consultations
  • To deliver materials you request (such as our renovation cost guide)
  • To understand how the Site is used and improve it
  • To communicate about your project

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. Information is shared only with the service providers listed above, to the extent needed to operate the Site.

Data retention

We keep inquiry information for as long as needed to serve your request and maintain our business records, then delete it. Analytics data is retained according to the respective providers' settings.

Your choices

  • You can ask us to access, correct or delete the information you submitted at any time — email hello@m5miami.com.
  • You can control cookies in your browser settings, and opt out of Google Analytics with the GA opt-out browser add-on.

Children

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the current version with its effective date is always at m5miami.com/privacy/.
