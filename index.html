FAQ

Questions we hear first

Do you subcontract the work?

Design, project management and supervision are in-house. Construction runs with our licensed Florida GC partner and our own vetted crews — one team accountable for the result.

Who handles permits and the condo board?

We do. Miami-Dade permits, COI for your building, elevator bookings and noise-hour rules are part of our process — not your problem.

How much does a project cost?

Turnkey design + renovation projects start around $200K. You get a clear itemized budget before you sign — and it doesn't change without a change order you approve first.

How long does it take?

A typical renovation takes up to 3 months. You get the timeline upfront and weekly photo updates all the way through.

Is there a warranty?

Yes — a 2-year written warranty. Any issue after handover is fixed by the same team that built it.
