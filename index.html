FAQ

Questions we hear first

Do you subcontract the work?

Design, project management and supervision are in-house. Construction runs with our licensed Florida GC partner and our own vetted crews. One team answers for the result.

Who handles permits and the condo board?

We do. Miami-Dade permits, COI for your building, elevator bookings and noise-hour rules are part of our process — not your problem.

How much does a project cost?

Turnkey design + renovation projects start around $130K. You see a clear itemized budget before you sign. It changes only through a change order you approve first.

How long does it take?

A typical renovation takes up to 3 months. You get the timeline upfront and weekly photo updates all the way through.

Is there a warranty?

Yes: a 1-year written warranty. The same team that built your project fixes any issue after handover.
