A whole-home renovation of a Coral Gables villa — double-height living, arched windows and travertine floors, brought into one warm, cohesive language.

The brief

Unify a house of disconnected rooms into a single, calm, family home that still feels grand.

Our approach

We reworked flow between the living spaces, restored the arched windows, laid travertine throughout, and layered linen, oak and plaster for warmth at scale.

The result

A full-cycle build delivered in 16 weeks with our licensed Florida GC, milestone payments and weekly updates.

What a Coral Gables villa renovation costs

Whole-home villa renovations in Coral Gables of this scale — roughly 2,580 sq ft — typically start around $375K, built full-cycle with our licensed Florida GC. Permits, milestone payments and inspections are handled in-house, with weekly photo updates all the way to handover.

Scope of work

  • Travertine flooring throughout
  • Restored arched windows
  • Custom joinery & millwork
  • Full-cycle build with licensed FL GC
