Our own studio showroom in Wynwood — a gallery-like space where clients see and touch the exact plaster textures, materials and finishes before a project starts.

The brief

Give clients certainty. No guessing from a screen — feel the real surface first.

Our approach

Sliding sample panels of Venetian plaster, microcement and limewash in metal frames, a materials library, a long working table and a lounge to plan projects.

The result

A space that turns 'I think I like it' into 'this is the one' — and doubles as our content studio.

See the finishes before you commit

Our Wynwood showroom is open by appointment — bring your plans or just your ideas. Touch real Venetian plaster, microcement and limewash samples, compare textures in Miami light, and leave knowing exactly how your walls will feel. Decorative plaster projects at M5 start from $10K.

Scope of work

  • Full plaster & material sample library
  • Sliding sample panels
  • Client planning lounge
  • Content & shoot space
  • By appointment — come and touch the finishes
