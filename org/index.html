The layer underneath everyone
JIN — infrastructure, not a colleague

Jin sits under the whole company, not next to anyone in it. It carries the routine for every person here: catches leads, answers in seconds, chases follow-ups, keeps Monday and Drive clean, reports the numbers.

It never touches what people are here for: the license and everything signed under it, judgment on money and scope, taste, and real relationships. Those do not get automated — they get protected by taking the paperwork off the people who own them.
